# Canting Chatbot - Hackathon Antigravity 2026 GDG Surabaya
CANTING (Catat Penting) is an innovative Agentic AI solution deployed on WhatsApp, designed specifically to transform the operations of Batik SMEs in Surabaya from complex manual systems into a "light and fast" digital ecosystem. Aligned with the Antigravity theme, this project addresses the challenge of operational drag—including manual inventory management, slow payment verification, and repetitive customer support—that currently hinders the productivity of 385,054 SMEs in Surabaya.
By leveraging the Google Cloud ecosystem, specifically Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), CANTING serves as an autonomous assistant with the authority to execute tasks independently. Its multimodal innovation allows it to process diverse inputs: sellers can update stock levels via voice notes, while the AI performs visual verification of payment proofs and product damage claims in real-time. Furthermore, the integration of RAG enables the "Batik Guide" feature to provide accurate, natural, and context-aware education regarding the philosophy and history of Surabaya batik motifs to customers.
This solution not only aims to provide seamless 24/7 customer support and instant operational efficiency but also serves as a strategic tool for cultural preservation. By removing technical and administrative burdens, CANTING empowers local artisans in centers like Kampung Batik Gundih to focus on their craft while remaining competitive in a global e-commerce market that reaches 88,1% of Indonesia's internet users
